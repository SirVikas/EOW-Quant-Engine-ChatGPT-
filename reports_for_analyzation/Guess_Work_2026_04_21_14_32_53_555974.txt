Is pure log files ka vistaar se vishleshan (analysis) karne ke baad, ye mukhya Alarming (chintajanak) bindu hain jo system ki stability aur trading performance ko prabhavit kar rahe hain:
1. Chronic Data Freshness Failure (Sabse bada khatra)

Poore log mein lagatar DATA_NOT_FRESH ki warning aa rahi hai.

    Stale Ticks: System ticks 60 seconds se zyada purane ho rahe hain (kuch jagah 100s+ aur 145s+ bhi hain).

    Gating Block: Iski wajah se Orchestrator lagatar GATE BLOCKED — no scan report kar raha hai, yaani system market ko scan hi nahi kar pa raha.

    Low Health Score: Data health score 71 se girkar lagatar 40 (Low Health) par ja raha hai.

2. Recursive Safe Mode Activation

Data freshness issues ki wajah se system baar-baar Safe Mode mein chala jata hai.

    Auto-Resume Cycle: System auto-resume hone ki koshish karta hai, lekin turant baad fir se DATA_NOT_FRESH detect karke Safe Mode mein chala jata hai. Ye ek "infinite loop" ki tarah dikh raha hai jo trading ko puri tarah rok deta hai.

3. Critical WebSocket Instability

WebSocket connection lagatar disconnect aur reconnect ho raha hai.

    No Ticks Received: Kayi baar 30-36 seconds tak koi tick nahi mil rahi, jis se connection "Stale" ho jata hai.

    Reconnect Attempts: Self-healing watchdog ko lagatar reconnect karna pad raha hai.

    Network Error: [Errno 11001] getaddrinfo failed jaisi error dikh rahi hai, jo DNS ya network connectivity issue ki taraf ishara karti hai.

4. Degraded Deployability Score

Boot aur runtime ke dauran Deployability Score hamesha "DEGRADED" rehta hai:

    Ye score 100/100 hona chahiye, lekin ye lagatar 45.0 se 66.5 ke beech ghoom raha hai.

    Reason: Indicators aur Data dono hi ready nahi hain (data=40 ind=0), jis se system trading ke liye anukul nahi maana ja raha.

5. Regime Classification Failures (UNKNOWN State)

Kayi assets (jaise AAVEUSDT, ETHUSDT) ke liye system regime classify nahi kar pa raha hai:

    UNKNOWN Regime: Regime classified as UNKNOWN dikh raha hai.

    Fallback Penalty: Classification na hone par system 70% confidence penalty ke saath pichle regime par fallback karta hai, jo ki galat trading signals de sakta hai.

6. Performance & Risk Concerns

    Drawdown (DD): Risk engine 13.6% ka drawdown report kar raha hai, jiski wajah se position size ko 50% tak kam kar diya gaya hai.

    Genome Drag: MeanReversion strategy mein 158% cost drag dikh raha hai, jo dikhata hai ki fees aur slippage profit ko khatam kar rahe hain.
